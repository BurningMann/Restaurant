Welcome To The Burning Snake

The restaurant "Burning Snake" has absorbed the best traditions of European, Spanish and Mediterranean cuisine. A cozy, friendly atmosphere, a kitchen and a worthy service are the main advantages of the restaurant. All of the above and plus an affordable price level allowed the institution to be on the list of the best restaurants in Belarus. The secret of popularity is simple - once here and feeling the cordiality, hospitality, warm welcome and thoughtful service, the guests will certainly come back again and recommend "Burning Snake" to their friends.
